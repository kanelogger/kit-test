# Workflow Knowledge Skill

Produce an onboarding plan from bundled policy references.

Workflow:

1. Identify the role and region.
2. Read `references/onboarding-policy.md`.
3. Draft a staged plan with tasks, owners, and policy citations.
4. Flag missing policy coverage instead of inventing facts.

Output contract:

- role
- region
- staged tasks
- cited policy sections
- missing information
